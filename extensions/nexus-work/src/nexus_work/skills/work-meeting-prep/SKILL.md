When a meeting is about to start:

1. Look up the calendar event for attendees and agenda
2. For each attendee, check recent interactions (emails, Slack, PRs)
3. Review open action items involving these people
4. Check delegations to/from attendees
5. List unresolved items from last meeting with these people

Present as a concise brief: who you're meeting, what's relevant, suggested talking points.
